Bill of Quantities (B.O.Q)

Ancient Contractors writes detailed bills of quantities.

A bill of quantities also known as B.O.Q is an estimate of the construction materials and other items that your construction project is likely to need in order to be completed.

Also note that the B.O.Q given to you is not 100% accurate and we don't guarantee that those are the final construction material needs. We help you estimate to our best for smoother construction.
